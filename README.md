🍔 Food Delivery Analytics Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

The Food Delivery Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze food delivery operations and business performance. It provides insights into revenue, order volume, delivery efficiency, and restaurant performance through dynamic visualizations and KPI metrics.

This dashboard helps businesses monitor key performance indicators, identify operational trends, and make data-driven decisions to improve customer satisfaction and overall business growth.

🎯 Objectives
Monitor overall business performance.
Analyze restaurant-wise revenue generation.
Track delivery efficiency and delivery times.
Compare restaurant performance.
Provide actionable insights through interactive visualizations.
📊 Key Features & Visual Insights
1. KPI Cards

🟢 Total Revenue – Total revenue generated from orders.

🔵 Order Count – Total number of orders placed.

🟣 Average Delivery Time – Average time taken for deliveries.

🟠 Total Delivery Time – Sum of delivery duration across all orders.

2. Revenue by Restaurant

Bar chart comparing revenue generated by each restaurant, helping identify top-performing restaurants.

3. Restaurant Revenue Distribution

Pie chart showing each restaurant's contribution to total revenue.

4. Amount by Restaurant

Column chart displaying revenue comparison across restaurants.

5. Delivery Time Analysis

Donut chart visualizing average delivery time across different orders.

7. Interactive Filters

🧮 DAX Measures Implemented
Total Revenue = SUM('Dirty_FoodDelivery_Analytics_900'[Amount])

Order Count = COUNT('Dirty_FoodDelivery_Analytics_900'[OrderID])

Average Delivery Time = AVERAGE('Dirty_FoodDelivery_Analytics_900'[DeliveryTime])

Delivery Status = Table.AddColumn(#"Trimmed Text", "Delivery Status", each if [DeliveryTime] > 30 then "Late" else "On Time")
